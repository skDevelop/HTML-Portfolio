Movies I Recommend

Three (3) Action Movies to Check Out

Terminator 1 & 2

A super computer sends back a killer robot through time to kill the savior of mankind, John Connor. Can the robot be stopped or will John lose his life.

Die Hard 1 - 3

A cop must risk life and limb to stop terrorist activities. See his struggle as he tries to prevent the worst from occurring.

The Matrix 1 - 3

Dive into a world inside the computer. Is this a tale of our soon coming future?
